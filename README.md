How do I interpret user needs and implement them into a program? How does creating “user stories” help with this?
  By creating and using User Stories, it proivdes the opportunity to interpret the user's wants/needs/desires regarding changes and their level of importance. Knowing these stories allows the programmer to ensure they understand the user prior to making the project so there is less of a         possibility of breaking changes going forward.

How do I approach developing programs? What Agile processes do I hope to incorporate into my future development work?
  First I get as much information as possible from the scrum master, aka my direct manager, and the product owner. 
  After I feel I have a direction to begin, I start small and work my way up. 
    Currently I am working on a project that is somewhat modeled after another project, but not enough to make a direct clone. So I am building it piece by piece from the ground up. 
  Always, test, test, test!
  Get it onto a Staging Server that mimics Production.
  Finally put onto production, and monitor until delivery.
  
What does it mean to be a good team member in software development?
  It means to be open and honest., communicate as well as possible, and always test before committing.
